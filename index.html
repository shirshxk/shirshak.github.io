About me

I am a versatile and dynamic professional with a unique combination of creative and technical skills, driven by a passion for cybersecurity and innovative design. With over 4 years of experience as a graphic design lead, I have successfully developed and executed content strategies that have elevated brand visibility and engaged diverse audiences. My work with organizations such as TEDxMaitighar and Footy.base has allowed me to merge my artistic vision with strategic execution, resulting in impactful social media content and merchandise designs.

Beyond my creative expertise, I am deeply passionate about the field of cybersecurity. Currently pursuing a degree in Ethical Hacking and Cybersecurity at Coventry University, I am committed to understanding and addressing the complexities of digital security in today’s interconnected world. I approach challenges with a blend of analytical thinking and technical precision, constantly seeking innovative solutions to safeguard digital environments. My knowledge extends to programming in C++, ethical hacking techniques, and project management, equipping me with a strong foundation to navigate both creative and technical domains.

As someone who thrives on continuous learning, I am driven by the belief that creativity and technology go hand-in-hand to solve real-world problems. Whether it’s designing captivating visuals or devising secure solutions to protect sensitive information, I am dedicated to making a meaningful impact. My journey reflects my curiosity, adaptability, and relentless pursuit of excellence in everything I do.
